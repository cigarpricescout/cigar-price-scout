SKU Normalization

Different retailers use different naming conventions for the same cigar. We normalize products using a standardized identifier system:

  • 1. Brand — Standardized manufacturer name (e.g., "Arturo Fuente")
  • 2. Line/Series — Product line matching (e.g., "Hemingway")
  • 3. Wrapper Type — Connecticut, Maduro, Habano, Cameroon, etc.
  • 4. Vitola — Shape name (Short Story, Robusto, Churchill, etc.)
  • 5. Size — Ring gauge × length (e.g., "4×49")
  • 6. Box Quantity — Number of cigars per box (e.g., "Box of 25")
Format: BRAND|LINE|VITOLA|SIZE|WRAPPER|BOX_QTY

Shipping Calculation

Estimated shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Retailer's published shipping policies and rates
  • Order subtotal (free shipping thresholds where applicable)
  • Destination state (derived from your ZIP code)

Note: Actual shipping may vary. Estimates are based on standard ground shipping to the contiguous United States.

Tax Calculation

Sales tax estimates consider:

  • State sales tax rates — Updated quarterly
  • Retailer nexus — Physical presence determines tax obligation
  • Tax base — Applied to subtotal + shipping

Note: Some states have additional tobacco taxes that may not be reflected. Always verify final tax at checkout.

Price Context Algorithm

We categorize prices relative to the market median to help you understand if you're getting a good deal:

Value 10% or more below median delivered price — an exceptional deal
Market Within 10% of median — fair market pricing
Premium 10% or more above median — consider alternatives

Authorized Dealer Designation

We distinguish between two types of retailers:

Authorized Dealers

Direct relationships with manufacturers. Products come with full manufacturer warranty coverage and verified authenticity.

Marketplaces

May offer competitive pricing but products may not include manufacturer warranty. Purchase at your own discretion.
