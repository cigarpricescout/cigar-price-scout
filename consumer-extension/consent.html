Welcome to Cigar Price Scout
We help cigar shoppers find the cheapest box price for the cigar they're looking at. To do that well, we crowdsource prices from the people doing the shopping — that could include you.
What we record when you opt in:
  • The cigar retailer page URL you're viewing (only on cigar retailer sites we already track).
  • The price, currency, and stock status shown on that page.
  • A random anonymous ID stored on your device (no email, no account, no name).
What we never record:
  • Personal info, account data, or anything you type into retailers.
  • Pages outside cigar retailer sites we track (homepage, cart, checkout, etc. are skipped).
  • Your IP beyond a short-term rate-limit check.
By continuing you confirm you're 21 or older. We never sell data and we don't run ads. Affiliate links to retailers may earn us a commission at no extra cost to you.
