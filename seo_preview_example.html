About Padron 1964 Anniversary

The Padron 1964 Anniversary is a premium Nicaraguan cigar celebrating Padron's 30th anniversary, originally released in 1994. This box-pressed masterpiece showcases exceptional craftsmanship and is available in both Natural and Maduro wrappers. In 2021, it achieved the prestigious Cigar of the Year honor with an outstanding 97-point rating, cementing its status among the finest cigars in the world.

Handcrafted using four-year aged Nicaraguan tobaccos, the 1964 Anniversary delivers a complex, full-bodied smoking experience that appeals to both connoisseurs and experienced enthusiasts. Each cigar is meticulously rolled to ensure perfect construction and consistent flavor throughout.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the Padron 1964 Anniversary special?
The 1964 Anniversary uses tobacco aged for a minimum of four years, resulting in exceptional smoothness and complexity. Its box-pressed format and meticulous construction make each cigar a premium smoking experience. The 2021 Cigar of the Year award (97 points) validates its world-class quality.
What's the difference between Natural and Maduro wrappers?
Natural: Features a lighter, sun-grown wrapper with notes of cedar, nuts, and cream. Slightly milder in strength.
Maduro: Uses a darker, fermented wrapper that adds sweetness, chocolate notes, and a richer overall profile. Both maintain the signature Padron complexity.
What vitolas are available?
Common vitolas include Exclusivo (5.5" x 50), Principe (4.5" x 46), and Diplomatico (7" x 50). Each size offers the same premium tobacco blend with slightly different smoking times and intensity levels.
